Who is Frederick?

Online knows as @ferdvisuals, my is Frederick Otto. I'm a photographer and short form video maker based in Berlin, Germany. On my Instagram account @ferdvisuals I am primarily known for my landscape phtography around the world. 5 years ago I fell in love with photography and it became my passion. I like to document places and tell their story through my photos. Whether I work for clients or discover my surroundings by my self, I'm striving to take the best possible photos. I believe, that you should never stop chasing your goals and all the negativities on your way are natural. I'm not afraid to show this in my photos and videos!
